Welcome to The Decentralized Social Network!


An application providing multiple benefits of a regular social networking website, but with the added benefits of lower latency and higher security provided by decentralization.


The various sections are:

  • 1) Profile: Where you manage your User Profile. Contains functionality to add friends and update your public profile that would be visible to all app users.
  • 2) Friend Posts: Where you send posts to your friends and read theirs for you.
  • 3) Groups: Where you can broadcast common messages to all your friends at once.
  • 4) Go To Chat: Platform to chat with your friends.
