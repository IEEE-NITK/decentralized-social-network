An application providing all the features of a regular social networking website, but with the added benefits of absolute privacy and control over your own data, without the involvement of third parties.


The various sections are:

  • 1) Profile: Where you manage your User Profile. Contains functionality to add friends and update your public profile that would be visible to all app users.
  • 2) Personal Posts: Where you send private posts to a friend and read their posts to you.
  • 3) Friends' Posts: Where you can broadcast common messages to all your friends at once.
  • 4) Start A Chat: Platform to chat with your friends.
